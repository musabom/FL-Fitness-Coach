Add "Am I On Track?" Card
Add a new card at the very top of the daily dashboard view, above the existing daily target number and all other sections. Do not touch anything below it.

The card
Title: AM I ON TRACK?
Three columns: CONSUMED — TARGET — VARIANCE
Five rows: Calories — burn line — Protein — Carbs — Fat

How each column is calculated
CONSUMED column:

Calories: food eaten today from meal plan completions
Burn line below calories: actual workout calories burned today from completed workouts. Show as −X kcal training in small muted grey. Only show if workouts were completed today.
Net calories: food eaten minus actual burned
Protein, Carbs, Fat: consumed from meal plan today

TARGET column:

Calories: food calorie target from the active plan
Burn line below calories: planned workout calories from workouts scheduled for today in the training plan. Show as −X kcal planned in small muted grey. Only show if workouts are scheduled today.
Net calories: food target minus planned burn
Protein, Carbs, Fat: macro targets from the active plan

VARIANCE column:

Net calories: consumed net minus target net
Protein, Carbs, Fat: consumed minus target for each macro
Negative = teal. Positive over target = amber. Over by more than 5% = red.
