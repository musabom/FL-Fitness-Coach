This is part of an existing project. Do not change anything already working. Add the three role system described below.

Three Types of Users
The app has three types of users — Member, Coach, and Admin. Every new user starts as a Member. Only the Admin can promote users to Coach. Only the Admin can assign clients to a Coach.

What the Member experiences
No change from what exists today. The member sees their own dashboard, meal plan, training plan, and progress. They cannot see any other user's data.
One new thing — if a coach has been assigned to them they see a small line on their dashboard: "Your coach: [name]"
When their coach updates their plan they see a banner at the top of their dashboard: "Your coach updated your plan."

What the Coach experiences
When a coach logs in they land on their client list — not their own personal dashboard.
The client list shows one card per assigned client. Each card shows the client's name, current weight, today's meal compliance, today's workout compliance, and their current goal.
Tapping a client opens that client's full dashboard — exactly what the client sees — with one addition: an Edit button in the top corner.
When the coach taps Edit they can:

Change the client's goal mode
Adjust the client's calorie deficit
Adjust the client's macro targets
Build or edit meals in the client's meal plan
Build or edit workouts in the client's training plan

Any change the coach makes is saved immediately and the client sees the update banner on their dashboard.
The coach also has access to their own personal dashboard by tapping My Dashboard in their navigation.

What the Admin experiences
When the admin logs in they land on the admin panel.
The admin panel has three sections:
Users
A list of all users showing name, email, role, join date, and current goal. The admin can tap any user and promote them to Coach or demote them back to Member.
Coaches
A list of all coaches. The admin can tap any coach and assign clients to them from the full member list. The admin can also remove a client from a coach.
Content
The admin can add, edit, and delete foods from the food database. The admin can add, edit, and delete exercises from the exercise library.

What happens when a member is promoted to Coach
Their role updates immediately. The next time they log in they see the coach view instead of their personal dashboard. Their own personal plan and data is preserved — they can still access it from My Dashboard.

Security
A member can never see another member's data. A coach can only see clients assigned to them — not other coaches' clients and not unassigned members. The admin can see everything.
